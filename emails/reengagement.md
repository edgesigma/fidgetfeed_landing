# Reengagement Email — Cold Waitlist

**To:** All existing waitlist signups (Nov 2024 – Jan 2026)
**Purpose:** Validate email addresses, confirm interest, warm up the list before beta launch

---

**Subject line options (pick one):**
1. still want in?
2. FidgetFeed is almost ready — are you still in?
3. quick question before we launch

---

**Body:**

Hey {{name|there}},

You signed up for FidgetFeed a while back — and I owe you an honest update.

Building my first Android app took way longer than I planned. What started as a few months turned into a full year of learning, scrapping, rebuilding, and getting it right. But the app is done, the beta is about to open, and I want to make sure you're still in.

Here's what FidgetFeed actually does now: when you reach for Instagram, TikTok, or whatever pulls you in, FidgetFeed steps in with something better — interactive fidgets that scratch the itch without the 45-minute scroll hole.

**I've got one question for you — just hit reply and tell me:**

> When you take a brain break, what do you usually reach for?
> (Scrolling socials? A game? Snacks? Staring at the wall? Something else?)

Your answer helps me make FidgetFeed better for you — and it confirms you still want early access. I'll personally make sure you're first in line when the beta drops.

Talk soon,
{{sender_name}}

P.S. If you're no longer interested, no worries at all — just ignore this and I won't email you again.

---

**Notes:**
- The P.S. serves as a passive unsubscribe / list cleaning mechanism. Anyone who doesn't reply within ~7 days can be marked inactive.
- The question maps directly to the branching onboarding flow ("what kind of break do you take?").
- Keep "from" name personal (first name, not "FidgetFeed Team") — it's a solo dev story and that's a strength.
