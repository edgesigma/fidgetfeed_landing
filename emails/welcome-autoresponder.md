# Welcome Autoresponder — New Waitlist Signups

**Trigger:** Immediately after beta signup form submission
**Purpose:** Confirm signup, build excitement, start a conversation

---

**Subject line options (pick one):**
1. you're on the list
2. you're in — one quick question
3. welcome to FidgetFeed (+ a question)

---

**Body:**

Hey {{name|there}},

You're on the closed beta list for FidgetFeed — thanks for signing up.

Here's the short version: FidgetFeed intercepts your most distracting apps and replaces them with interactive fidgets. You get the dopamine hit your brain wanted — without the scroll hole.

The beta is opening very soon, and you'll be among the first to try it.

**In the meantime, I'm curious — hit reply and tell me:**

> When you reach for your phone on a break, what do you usually end up doing?

I read every reply. Your answer helps me shape the experience so it actually works for you.

Talk soon,
{{sender_name}}

---

**Notes:**
- Keep it short. They just signed up — don't overwhelm.
- The reply question mirrors the reengagement email, feeding the same onboarding data.
- "I read every reply" signals this is a real person, not a faceless startup. Follow through on that.
- Can be sent via any ESP (Mailchimp, SendGrid, Resend, etc.) or even a simple Google Apps Script triggered by the form submission.
